Travel to London

Things to see:

  • Big Ben

    Big Ben is one of London's most historic sites. Contrary to popular belief, it is not the name of the clock tower itself, but of the bell inside it. It weighs 13 and a half tons and was the biggest bell in the UK for 23 years.

  • The London Eye

    The London Eye is a giant Ferriis wheel in London. It's 135 meters tall and gives you a great view of the city. Tourisis will definitely want to check this out.

  • Piccadilly Circus

    Picadilly Circus is basically London's equivalent of Times Square. A lot of major shopping areas are located within it. The most notable thing about its appearance is the giant billboards on the side of a building. If you want to get some shopping done in London, this is the place to go.

Watch out for:

  1. Rain
  2. Crazy drivers
  3. People in Speaker's Corner
